Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

FoodLens ("the App") is developed by Yingying Zhu ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Images You Scan

When you scan a product label, the image is sent to our server for ingredient analysis. We do not permanently store your images on our servers. Images are processed in real-time and discarded after analysis is complete.

Scan History

Your scan history (product names, ingredients, and analysis results) is stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly initiate a scan.

Purchase Information

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing addresses. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA) or use advertising frameworks
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

The only data processing we perform is analyzing product ingredient images to provide you with ingredient information, health scores, and safety assessments. This processing occurs in real-time and no personal data is retained on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Open Food Facts — We query the Open Food Facts public database to retrieve product information by barcode. This is a public, open-source database and no personal data is shared with them.

Apple StoreKit — Subscription purchases are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

5. Data Storage and Security

All scan history and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We do not maintain user accounts or cloud databases containing your personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete your scan history at any time from the Settings screen within the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
